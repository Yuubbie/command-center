The 10 System Rules

Read these every Sunday night. Break them and the system collapses.

01
School before everything else each morning.
Not WhatsApp. Not football highlights. Not church admin. School first — every single day until exams are done.
02
Pick your Daily 3 the night before.
Not in the morning when you're tired. Decide while you still have energy — before you sleep.
03
Every task gets written down the moment it enters your head.
Stop trusting your memory. You have too much going on. Write it down immediately — phone notes, paper, anything.
04
SIWES logbook updated every day from 8 June.
Even 10 minutes counts. Never let it pile up — it becomes impossible to catch up and you'll lose marks.
05
Every Sunday evening is your Weekly Reset — 20 minutes.
Review what happened. Plan the coming week. Update all trackers. This keeps the whole system alive.
06
Church music song must be ready by Wednesday. Not Friday.
Wednesday deadline removes Sunday morning panic entirely. Lead with excellence, not last-minute stress.
07
Family time 8–10am is protected daily.
The kids and your wife get you — not your phone, not your laptop. Be fully present. This is non-negotiable.
08
Sleep by 1am at the latest on Uber nights.
A tired Ubong learns nothing and earns less. You cannot study on 2 hours of sleep. Protect your sleep.
09
One freelance application per day. Just one.
Consistent beats frantic. One good application daily = 30 this month. Quality over panic-applying.
10
Every Sunday: update register, file report, plan next week.
30 minutes total. Church admin fully done before Monday starts. Never carry it into the week.
